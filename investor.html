07/Demand drivers

The income story has a manufacturing-led demand tailwind.

Three structural drivers underpin Crestview MOB demand over the next decade:

Williams International advanced aviation campus. A $1B manufacturing facility 7 minutes north of the property, anchoring Florida's largest industrial park (Shoal River Ranch, 10,500 acres). 4,000+ projected direct manufacturing jobs over 10–15 years.

Residential pipeline. 6,387 housing units approved or under construction in the Crestview MSA. Population growth supports outpatient healthcare demand across all service lines.

Military anchor. Eglin AFB (largest US military base by land area) and Hurlburt Field (AFSOC headquarters) sustain $9.18B in annual regional economic impact and a consistent military + retiree healthcare population.

The FDOT bypass system (under construction, multiple phases) is reshaping commercial corridors in ways that benefit the property's location and access profile.
